How to Use Markers

Marker A (Red): Click on the chart at your first measurement point to place the red marker. This marks the start of your measurement.

Marker B (Blue): Click on a second point in the chart to place the blue marker. The area between the markers will be highlighted.

Clear Markers: Use the "Clear Cursors" button to remove both markers and start a new measurement.
